Cormorant Garamond — Display / Wordmark

Cormorant Garamond brings centuries of typographic refinement to the Convene brand — authoritative in headlines, elegant at any weight. Its subtle contrast between thick and thin strokes echoes the precision of legal and governance documents.

Jost — Interface / Supporting Text

Jost provides the clean geometric counterpoint — equally at home as a navigation label, a form hint, or a data caption. Its near-perfect circular geometry complements the mark's own arc construction.
