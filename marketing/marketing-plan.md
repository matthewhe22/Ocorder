**Version:** 1.0

## 1. Executive Summary

The **TOCS OC Order Portal** is a self-service digital platform that enables property owners, conveyancers, lawyers, and real estate professionals to order Owner Corporation (OC) certificates and strata documents online — anytime, without phone calls or manual paperwork.

This marketing plan outlines how to position, promote, and grow adoption of the platform within the Australian property and strata services market.

---

## 2. Product Overview

### What is the OC Order Portal?

A purpose-built web platform that digitises the end-to-end process of ordering Owner Corporation documentation, including:

- OC Certificates (standard & urgent)
- Building Register Searches
- Insurance Certificates of Currency
- Meeting Minutes & Financial Statements
- Keys, Fobs & Access Remotes (with delivery)

### Core Value Proposition

> **"Order your OC certificates online in minutes — no phone calls, no delays, no paperwork."**

- Available 24/7 online
- Transparent pricing
- Real-time order tracking
- Secure document delivery by email
- Supports standard and urgent turnaround

## 3. Market Analysis

### 3.1 Industry Context

Australia's strata property sector is one of the fastest growing in the world, with over 320,000 strata schemes registered nationally (UNSW City Futures Research Centre). The conveyancing and property transaction sector processes hundreds of thousands of settlements annually, each typically requiring OC certificates.

**Key trends driving demand:**
- Rapid growth in apartment and townhouse living
- Increasing digital adoption across legal and property sectors
- Conveyancers seeking faster, self-service document ordering
- Property buyers demanding faster settlement timelines
- State government focus on strata transparency and disclosure

### 3.2 Target Market Segments

| Segment | Description | Pain Point Solved |
|---|---|---|
| **Conveyancers & Solicitors** | Handle OC certificate orders for property settlements daily | Manual ordering, slow turnaround, phone queues |
| **Real Estate Agents** | Need OC docs for vendor disclosure & contracts | Delays at listing/settlement stage |
| **Property Owners** | Individual lot owners needing certificates for refinancing or sale | Unsure where to go, complex process |
| **Building Managers** | Strata managers ordering docs for multiple clients | Batch orders, admin inefficiency |

### 3.3 Competitive Landscape

Most OC certificate orders in Australia are still handled via:
- Phone and email with strata managers
- Manual PDF forms submitted by post or email
- Through lawyers/conveyancers as intermediaries

**TOCS OC Portal's competitive advantages:**
- Fully self-service — available 24/7
- Real-time order confirmation and tracking
- Multiple payment options (card, bank transfer, PayID, invoice)
- Urgent turnaround options available
- Secure authority document upload
- Direct email delivery of certificates

## 6. Marketing Strategy

### 6.1 Channel Strategy

#### Digital Marketing

**Search Engine Optimisation (SEO)**
- Target keywords: "OC certificate order online", "owner corporation certificate NSW", "strata certificate conveyancer", "OC search Australia"
- Create blog content: guides on OC certificates, strata property FAQs, conveyancer resources
- Local SEO for Sydney and Melbourne strata markets

**Google Ads (SEM)**
- Run paid search campaigns targeting high-intent keywords
- Conversion goal: portal registrations and completed orders
- Retargeting ads for site visitors who didn't complete an order

**LinkedIn Marketing**
- Target: Conveyancers, property lawyers, strata managers, real estate principals
- Sponsored posts highlighting time-saving features
- Case studies from early adopter firms

**Email Marketing**
- Monthly newsletter to registered users (order tips, product updates, strata news)
- Onboarding drip campaign for new registrations
- Re-engagement campaigns for inactive users

#### Industry & Partnership Marketing

**Conveyancer Associations**
- Australian Institute of Conveyancers (AIC) chapters — VIC, NSW, QLD
- Sponsor e-newsletters and member emails
- Present at chapter meetings and professional development events

**Law Firm Partnerships**
- Approach property law practices (boutique and mid-size)
- Offer bulk/firm-level accounts with invoice payment terms
- Create referral incentive for firms driving high order volume

**Real Estate Network**
- Engage Real Estate Institute of Australia (REIA) and state-level bodies
- Target property management and sales departments
- Offer integration with popular CRM/property management tools (Rex, PropertyMe, Console)

**Strata Community Partnerships**
- Strata Community Association (SCA) sponsorship
- Feature in strata industry publications and directories

#### Content Marketing

**Resource Centre / Blog Topics:**
- "What is an OC Certificate and when do you need one?"
- "How to order an OC certificate in Australia (2026 guide)"
- "Strata property purchase checklist for conveyancers"
- "Understanding owner corporation levies and their impact on property value"
- "Top 5 document mistakes that delay property settlements"

**Video Content:**
- 90-second product demo: how to place an OC order in 3 steps
- Explainer: what's included in an OC certificate
- Testimonial videos from conveyancers and property professionals

### 6.2 Launch Campaign — "Order Smarter"

**Campaign Theme:** *"OC documents shouldn't slow down your settlement."*

**Launch Phases:**

| Phase | Duration | Activity |
|---|---|---|
| **Phase 1: Soft Launch** | Weeks 1–4 | Onboard TOCS existing customers; gather testimonials; fix UX issues |

**Launch Offer:**

## 12. Risk & Mitigation

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Low adoption by conveyancers (habit change) | Medium | High | Offer free trials; create firm-level onboarding; demonstrate ROI |
| Competitor platforms entering market | Low | High | Build strong brand loyalty; develop integrations early |
| Platform reliability issues affecting trust | Low | High | SLA monitoring; status page; proactive comms |
| Data/privacy concerns from users | Low | Medium | Publish clear privacy policy; GDPR/Australian Privacy Act compliance |
| Pricing pressure from manual competitors | Medium | Low | Emphasise speed, convenience, self-service value |
